Clove hitch

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The clove hitch is a type of knot. Along with the bowline and the sheet bend, it is often considered one of the most important knots.

It can be used as a binding knot, but is not particularly secure in that role.

A clove hitch made around the rope's own standing part is known as either two half-hitches.
